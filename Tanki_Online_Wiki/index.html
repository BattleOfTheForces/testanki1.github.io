Challenge accepted January I 2023

It turns out that the Challenge doesn’t end when you reach 3000 stars!

It’s time to share the results of the previous Challenge Accepted event and launch a new one! Let us remind you that during each challenge we give you an opportunity to win even more prizes for tankers whose star counter is always over 3000. 

The «Crisis XT» drone was the super prize of the previous event!

The list of winners is available in this spreadsheet: https://bit.ly/3i3Ylvl 

And now we are again changing the prizes for TOP-10 players and announcing a new Challenge. Players with the highest number of stars earned will get the following prizes:
  • TOP-1 — «Hyperion XT» drone
  • TOP-10 — Thunder’s «Hyperspeed» shells» augment
  • TOP-100 — 100 Tankoins
  • TOP-1000 — 300 batteries

If a winner already owns their prize, the prize doesn’t get deferred to the next TOP player. 

The rules are simple: earn stars during the new challenge. 

Track your progress and see the list of the TOP-100 players on the special page.

Good luck!
