Tanki Online and Loss of control

Hello, tankers!

Time to reveal all the secrets…

Tanki Online has its own laboratory. Under the close supervision of scientists, incredible experiments are conducted — new tank items are grown and sublimated.

We also have to admit that something irreparable has happened… A particularly dangerous virus has been leaked. According to our calculations, as early as this Friday, at 2 AM UTC on December 16th, there will be a mass infection of players!

But we have good news. We managed to develop an antidote. It is not a simple task to deliver it to the mechanisms of each tank and we won’t be able to do it without your help. 

We are launching a new event called «Tanki Online and Loss of control».

How to take part

To take part in the «Tanki Online and Loss of control» event, you will need to purchase the «Special access» offer.

Once you have the access pass, you will be added to one of the two factions — «Zombies» or «Immunes».




























  





If you are not satisfied with the distribution, you can change your faction by purchasing the special «Defector» offer, that includes:










  • Change of faction
  • 30 000 crystals





 





With the start of the event, the special website will be launched. There you will see your personal progress and the progress of your faction.





 





Event rules





 





After distribution, the main task of both factions is to get as many Test Tubes as possible. «What are these Test Tubes for?» you may ask. That’s simple, they are the only way for Zombies to transfer their infected blood and for Immunes to transfer the antidote. 





Test Tubes are the special event currency. You can get it for completing a set of special missions, purchase them in the Shop for crystals, or buy a special offer for Tankoins.



















 





Special missions





 





Only players who purchased the «Special access» offer will get access to a set of special missions.





We have prepared a lot of them, and each new day you will get a new mission. You have until the end of the event to complete them. The more missions you complete, the more personal prizes you will get and the more chances your faction will have of winning!










You will have time to complete the missions and get Test Tubes from 2 AM UTC on December 16th till 2 AM UTC on January 9th.

Packs of Test Tubes





 









 





For the duration of the event, there will be 3 packs of Test Tubes available in the Shop:










  • Test Tubes х1
  • Test Tubes х3
  • Test Tubes х5





 





You can purchase these packs for crystals.





 





Special offer





 





This special offer can help you to make a significant contribution to the victory of your faction!





 





Special «Laboratory container» offer









  • 15 Ultra Containers
  • 1 day of Premium Pass
  • 12 Test Tubes





 





Rewards





 





There are multiple sets of prizes! A lot to fight for!





 





Team competition





 





The faction whose members have the most Test Tubes by the end of the event wins and gets the main prize – the Freeze «Legacy» skin.









  • All Test Tubes that a player earns from the start of the event are counted in favour of the current faction of the player.
  • When a player changes faction, all Test Tubes that a player earned from the start of the event are moved to the new faction together with the player.





 





Participation of each player is necessary for victory!





 





Individual competition





 





  • Each participant gets a set of personal tasks. Completing them contributes towards your personal progress in the event. 
  • The more Test Tubes you have (the more tasks are completed), the more prizes you can get.
  • A player gets rewards for each task that is marked as completed at the end of the event (all prizes are mentioned on the website): a lot of Containers, crystals, supplies, batteries, the «Vulture» Mk7 module with protection from Scorpion, the «Helios» augment for Ricochet, the «Vacuum shells» augment for Thunder, units of the «Nuclear energy» supply, and a Skin Container.
  • At the moment of a faction change, the player’s personal progress is reset to zero. Test Tubes earned before the faction change are not taken into account in the player’s personal progress, and completed tasks are marked as incomplete.





 





Important: All prizes are issued after the end of the event.





 





Good luck to all tankers! And may the best man win!
